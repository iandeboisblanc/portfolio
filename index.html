About Me

I am a full-stack engineer specializing in Javascript and with a strong background in data analytics. Often guided by my curiosity, I've built front-end UI for web, mobile, and VR; implemented backend models and APIs in several languages; and designing and erecting databases and ETL pipelines.

Tech that I have used and become familiar with includes React, Angular, Backbone, React Native, Ionic, Aframe-VR, Redux, Socket.io, Node, Express, Hapi, GraphQL, Ruby on Rails, SQL, MongoDB, Neo4j, Amazon Redshift, Docker, Python, and more.

In my free time, I continuously seek new creative outlets and tinkering projects. I love to unwind with a few days in the woods, a fly rod, and a tasty beer.
